Ingredients:





To prepare Chicken Tikka, you will need 500 grams of boneless chicken, 1 cup yogurt, 1

tablespoon ginger-garlic paste, 1 teaspoon red chili powder, 1 teaspoon turmeric

powder, 1 teaspoon cumin powder, 1 teaspoon garam masala, 1 tablespoon lemon juice,

and salt according to taste. These ingredients work together to create the rich, spicy,

and delicious flavor that makes Chicken Tikka one of the most popular dishes. Using

fresh ingredients and the right balance of spices helps ensure the best taste and texture

in the final dish.
